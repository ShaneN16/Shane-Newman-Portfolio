01 / About

Professional
Introduction

I improve performance through people, systems, and disciplined execution.

During 22 years in the United States Marine Corps, I have led operations, logistics, workforce development, talent selection, and organizational transformation in high-pressure, highly regulated environments.

My work follows a consistent pattern: identify what is limiting performance, determine whether the problem sits with people, process, structure, or decision rights, and build an operating mechanism that leaders can sustain.

I have directed operations for organizations of up to 568 personnel, managed assets valued at $372 million, developed a transportation strategy that avoided $700,000 in projected cost, redesigned a manual enrollment process that now reclaims more than 1,100 staff hours annually, and designed a formal seven-stage instructor-selection process where no standardized system previously existed.

I am pursuing senior leadership and consulting opportunities involving organizational effectiveness, operations transformation, workforce strategy, leadership development, and strategy execution.
